Lance Scarrott - Groom

Recipe for a Lance

3 cups of patience
350g of affection
1 tablespoon curiosity
2 teaspoons dried wit
a pinch of charm

- put the affection in a saucepan and heat until warm and melts your heart

- slowly combine patience, curiosity and dried wit and mix until mixture turns into a smooth batter

- pour into a male shaped bread tin

- bake until golden

- can be served with or without pants

Recipe for a Lea

2 cups of pure energy
250g of love
1 large cute smile
2 teaspoons cartwheels
a dash of pragmatism

- mix energy and love in a large bowl until smooth

- fold in other ingredients until well combined

- take mixture and pour into a fun shaped tin

- cook in oven, no need to take out as she will jump out when ready with a pose and yell "TADA!"

- best served with a pokemon cosplay
